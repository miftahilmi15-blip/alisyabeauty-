Buat halaman "HOME" untuk aplikasi salon premium Alisya Beauty.

Tujuan:
Halaman HOME berfungsi sebagai landing utama yang menampilkan branding, layanan utama, promo, testimoni, jam operasional, dan mendorong user menuju booking.

---

Tema:
- Luxury Muslimah Beauty Salon
- Dark elegant background (#050507)
- Gold premium accent (#d4af37)
- Rose gold soft accent (#e8a7a1)
- UI modern, clean, mobile-first
- Tampilan seperti aplikasi VIP salon / skincare premium

---

STRUKTUR HOME:

1. TOP HEADER
- Logo Alisya Beauty
- Nama user (Firebase Auth)
- Icon profile di kanan atas
- Klik profile → ke halaman Profil

---

2. HERO SECTION (MAIN BANNER)
- Judul: "Alisya Beauty"
- Subjudul: "Luxury Muslimah Beauty Experience"
- Deskripsi singkat branding
- Tombol utama:
  - "Book Now" (primary CTA)
  - "Explore Services" (secondary CTA)
- Background elegan dengan efek glow gold

---

3. QUICK SERVICES (4 KATEGORI UTAMA)
Tampilkan dalam card grid 2x2:

- Hair Treatment
- Face Treatment
- Body Treatment
- Spa Treatment

Setiap card:
- Glassmorphism style
- Hover animation (float + glow gold)
- Klik → menuju halaman layanan atau booking

---

4. PROMO SECTION
- Banner promo dengan desain premium
- Contoh:
  "Special Offer - Diskon 20% Hair Spa"
- Efek glass + gold glow

---

5. POPULAR TREATMENT
- Menampilkan layanan paling sering dibooking
- Card horizontal scroll
- Contoh:
  Hair Spa, Facial Glow, Body Massage

---

6. TESTIMONI / REVIEW
- Tampilkan rating bintang (⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐)
- 3–5 review customer terbaik
- Nama user + komentar singkat
- Desain card glassmorphism

---

7. GOOGLE MAPS LOCATION
- Embed lokasi salon
- Tombol "Open in Maps"
- Tampilan clean di card

---

8. 🕒 JAM OPERASIONAL (NEW SECTION - IMPORTANT)
- Card informasi jam buka salon
- Data dinamis dari Firebase settings/operational

Contoh tampilan:
🕒 Open Hours
Senin - Minggu
09:00 - 21:00

FITUR:
- Jika hari libur → tampil "Closed"
- Bisa diupdate dari admin settings tanpa coding

Desain:
- Glassmorphism card
- Border gold tipis
- Icon jam di kiri
- Smooth fade animation

---

9. FINAL CTA SECTION
- Text:
  "Ready to Glow with Us?"
- Button:
  "Book Appointment Now"
- Animasi glow gold

---

UI STYLE:

- Glassmorphism cards
- Border gold tipis
- Shadow premium soft
- Hover animation (scale + glow)
- Smooth scroll reveal animation
- Dark mode default
- Font modern (Poppins / Inter)
- Font Awesome icons

---

ANIMASI WAJIB:

- Scroll reveal (fade + slide up)
- Card hover float effect
- Button glow animation
- Smooth transition antar section

---

INTERAKSI:

- Klik "Book Now" → ke halaman booking
- Klik kategori → ke layanan detail
- Klik profile → ke halaman profil
- Maps bisa dibuka ke Google Maps

OUTPUT YANG DIINGINKAN:

- HTML struktur HOME

  - scroll animation
  - navigation handler
